About

Hello! I'm Alex Jonic, a Computer Science major and aspiring game developer.

I've had a fascination with video games ever since I was young,
with some of my earliest childhood memories being that of watching my dad play Tomb Raider and King's Quest.
Since then, I've grown to appreciate the video game industry on a deeper, more technical level
as I began to pursue a career in game development.

I'm currently finishing up my Bachelors of Science at Lewis University, with plans to gradute in Spring 2019.

In my down time, I a work as a freelance digital artist,
and can often be found working on my own personal video game
projects using Unity and Unreal Engine.
